Endangered Language
Transcription and Translation



Our Aim

Building dialect recognition tools and Automatic Speech Recognition for endangered low-resource languages for preserving it's cultural heritage, language documentation, linguistic research, education and communication.

Features Of Our System

Many of these language doesn't have any written forms.Therefore transcribing it to the written form in same exact language is essential.
And the converting the transcribed dialect into a standardized version of that language.
Finaly translating the transcribed text into another language.

Behind The Scenes

Transfer learning and fine-tuning methodologies are adopted for low-resource languages and also for languages that do not have any written forms. The languages which do not have any written form are transcribed into most similar languages.

Who Are We

Virtual Resource Centre for Language Computing (VRCLC) is established at IIITM-K to promote language computing research and development to bridge the digital divide and leverage the power of Information Technology.
